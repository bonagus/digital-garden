**All you need to know even if won’t guarantee your happiness or succesfull life, but it sure can help :**

**1. Practice thinking about your thinking.**

> Who looks outside, dreams; who looks inside, awakes.
>
> ― Carl Jung

**2. Be compassionate with your mistakes.**

> You’ve been criticising yourself for years and it hasn’t worked. Try approving of yourself and see what happens.
>
> ― Louise Hay

**3. Cultivate a healthy skepticism of your thoughts.**

> At a child’s birth, if a mother could ask a fairy godmother to endow it with the most useful gift, that gift would be curiosity.
>
> ― Eleanor Roosevelt

**4. Learn to accept your emotions.**

> The moment we cry in a film is not when things are sad but when they turn out to be more beautiful than we expected them to be.
>
> ― Alain de Botton

**5. Take responsibility for your actions.**

> Most people do not really want freedom, because freedom involves responsibility, and most people are frightened of responsibility.
>
> ― Sigmund Freud

**6. Distinguish desires from values.**

> The price of getting what you want, is getting what you once wanted.
>
> ― Neil Gaiman

**7. Have the humility to ask for help when you need it.**

> There is nothing noble in being superior to your fellow man; true nobility is being superior to your former self.
>
> ― Ernest Hemingway
